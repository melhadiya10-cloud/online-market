Our Story

Born in Ethiopia.
Built for the World.

It started with a simple realization: finding authentic, high-quality cosmetics in Ethiopia shouldn't feel like a treasure hunt. Like so many of you, I knew the frustration of running out of a favorite product, the uncertainty of buying from unfamiliar sources, and the endless search for something that actually works. That frustration became fuel, and Nardis was born. We don't just sell products we curate an experience. Every item in our collection is chosen with intention, from the nourishing formulas of Cantu and Mielle to the science backed serums of The Ordinary, the timeless elegance of CeraVe, and the captivating notes of fragrances like 9PM and Victoria. We travel the world to bring the best to your doorstep, because we believe that distance shouldn't determine access to excellence. But Nardis is more than a beauty shop. It's a promise. A promise that you'll always find authenticity here no fakes, no shortcuts, no compromises. It's a promise that we'll treat your needs like our own, offering honest advice and personalized recommendations because we genuinely care. It's a commitment to community, to elevating the standard of beauty in Ethiopia, and to proving that luxury can be both accessible and real. Every order is packed with care, delivered with speed, and backed by a team that lives and breathes the same beauty struggles you do. Because we're not just building a brand we're building trust. One product, one customer, one story at a time.
